# Product

This is a personal portfolio website for Sumedha Gupta, a software engineer and CS student at ASU (Class of 2027).

The site is a single-page application with smooth scroll navigation. It showcases:
- Professional experience and projects
- Technical skills and leadership
- Education background
- Contact information

The design aesthetic is terminal/developer-themed with animated elements, a typewriter effect, rotating word headlines, and a live terminal window in the hero section. The owner is open to internships (2026).
